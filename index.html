Two Modes Dwa tryby

Classic Mode gives you full control with exercise tabs, set tables, notes, and RPE tracking. Focus Mode shows one exercise at a time with large buttons and swipe navigation — perfect for training in gloves. Tryb klasyczny daje pełną kontrolę z zakładkami ćwiczeń, tabelami serii i RPE.
